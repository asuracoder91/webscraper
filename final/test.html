About the role:

Injective Labs is seeking a talented and passionate Product Designer to join our growing team. As a Product Designer, you will play a crucial role in designing intuitive and user-friendly interfaces for the Injective Protocol platform. You will work closely with our product managers, engineers, and other stakeholders to understand user needs, translate them into engaging design solutions, and ensure a seamless user experience across all aspects of our platform.
This is a global remote role.

Responsibilities:

    • Work end-to-end design projects, collaborate with product managers and engineers to understand requirements.
    • Gather user feedback, advocate for our users, and analyze user behavior to inform design decisions.
    • Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups to visualize and communicate design ideas.
    • Design intuitive and visually appealing interfaces for web and mobile applications.
    • Ensure consistency in design language, branding, and user experience across all products and platforms.
    • Iterate on designs based on user feedback, business needs, and data analysis.
    • Work closely with developers to implement designs and provide guidance throughout the development process.
    • Stay updated on industry trends, best practices, and emerging technologies in design and user experience.
    • Build and maintain our design system.
